## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
- An active email address for account verification
- Node.js `{version >= 18}` installed (for local development)
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Log In

Create your AlisX account to access the platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to [https://app.alisx.com](https://app.alisx.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Register Account" icon="user-plus">
    Enter your email, create a password, and verify your account via the email link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the dashboard and sign in with your credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Project

Once logged in, start a new project using the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Project" icon="plus">
    Click **New Project** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Basics" icon="settings">
    Enter a project name like `my-first-app`, select **AI-Native Web App** as the template, and choose your preferred stack (React or Next.js).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Wizard" icon="sparkles">
    Follow the initial setup wizard:
    
    1. Define your app's core functionality (e.g., "Task management dashboard").
    2. Enable AI code generation.
    3. Review and confirm settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Deploy Your Application

Deploy your project with one click.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Dashboard Deploy" icon="upload-cloud">
    In your project dashboard:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Build & Deploy">
        Click **Deploy** > **Production**. AlisX handles building, optimization, and hosting.
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Custom Domain" default-open="false">
        Add your domain in **Settings > Domains** and verify DNS.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="CLI Deploy" icon="terminal">
    Install the AlisX CLI first:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="npm,yarn">
    ````bash
    npm install -g @alisx/cli
    ````
    
    ````bash
    yarn global add @alisx/cli
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
    
    Then deploy:
    
    ````bash
    alisx login
    alisx deploy
    ````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Your app deploys to a unique URL like `https://my-first-app.alisx.app`.
