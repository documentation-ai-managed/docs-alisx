## Quick Start

1. Sign up at `https://dashboard.alis.exchange`
2. Confirm your email
3. Log in to your dashboard

## Create Your First Project

1. Click "New Project"
2. Select "AI Solution" for automated workflows
3. Name your project and choose a template

## Tips

Use the left menu to explore deployments, models, and monitoring tools. Reach out via support for guided onboarding if needed.
