## Overview

Use AlisX to deploy an AI model with just a few clicks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Go to the dashboard and click "New Project".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your model">
    Drag and drop your AI model file, or select a template model.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure settings">
    Set parameters like hardware and scaling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy">
    Click "Deploy". Your model will be available via AlisX API endpoint.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once deployed, monitor status and logs under the "Deployments" tab.
