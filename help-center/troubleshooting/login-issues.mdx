---
title: Login Issues on AlisX
description: How to resolve problems signing in to AlisX.
---

## Symptoms

- Cannot access dashboard
- Error: "Invalid credentials" or "Account not found"

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset your password">
    Start at `https://dashboard.alis.exchange/reset-password`
    and follow the instructions sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your email address">
    Make sure you use the address registered with AlisX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Clear cookies and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the problem continues, contact support at support@alis.exchange.
